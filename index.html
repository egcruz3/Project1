About Survivor Project
In a world where victims and survivors of sexual assault are continuously silenced, denied, and blamed (as most recently perpetuated by the Trump Administration's rescinding of protections for victims on college campuses), we, as a group of survivors, activists, and web developers, have created this digital space for all victims and survivors to share our stories anonymously, make our voices heard, know we are not alone, take action for a world free from sexual assault.
